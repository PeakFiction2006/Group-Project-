  1. Network Administrator

    • Reference Number: NAXG2
    • Salary: $85,00 - $105,000 AUD (source)
    • Years of experience: MINIMUM 2 years

      • Brief Description

      We're looking for a proactive and detail-oriented Network Administrator to join our IT team. In this role, you'll be responsible for managing and maintaining our network infrastructure, ensuring the security, performance, and reliability of systems that support our day-to-day operations.

    • Responsibilities of a Network Administrator at CORPORATION

      1. Network Installation and Configuration
        • Set up and configure routers, switches, firewalls, and wireless access points
        • Install and maintain networking software and hardware

      2. Monitoring and Performance Management
        • Continuously monitor network performance
        • Use tools like SolarWinds, Nagios, or Wireshark to identify issues
        • Ensure uptime and optimize speed and efficiency of the network

      3. Troubleshooting and Support
        • Respond to network outages and technical issues
        • Provide support to end-users experiencing connectivity problems
        • Diagnose and resolve hardware, software, and connectivity issues

      4. Security Management
        • Implement firewalls and security protocols
        • Manage antivirus, access controls, and VPN configurations
        • Perform regular security audits and updates to guard against breaches

      5. User Account and Access Control
        • Manage permissions, access rights, and user accounts
        • Monitor network usage to prevent unauthorized access or misuse

      6. Network Documentation and Reporting
        • Maintain accurate documentation of configurations and procedures
        • Track inventory of hardware and licenses
        • Generate performance and incident reports for management

      7. Data Backup and Recovery
        • Ensure regular backups of network configurations and critical data
        • Create and test disaster recovery plans

      8. Upgrades and Maintenance
        • Install patches, updates, and firmware
        • Recommend and implement hardware/software upgrades
        • Plan and test new networking technologies

      9. Collaboration and Training
        • Work with IT teams, vendors, and service providers
        • Train staff on basic networking tools and safety practices

      10. Compliance and Best Practices
        • Ensure the network complies with organizational policies and industry standards (e.g., ISO, HIPAA, GDPR depending on industry)

    • Key Network Administrator Skills

      • Knowledge of different types of networks: Network types include Local Area Networks (LANs), Wide Area Networks(WANs), Virtual Private Networks (VPNs), and sometimes Storage Area Networks (SANs). Though some jobs may require more experience in one over another, having a basic understanding of the various network types and how to configure them will be crucial to your network administrator skill set.

      • Understanding of network security components: These include firewalls, VPNs, and access control. Knowing about common cyber attacks and the appropriate response to them is critical for a network administrator.

      • Familiarity with servers: Upgrading and configuring servers is a sought-after skill for network administrators. These may include Windows and Linux operating systems.

      • Communication and teamwork: As a network administrator, you'll likely work on a team or with other organization members to ensure computers and systems are running smoothly. Being able to communicate problems and solutions with other people is a valuable skill to have.

    • Essential Qualifications and Skills

      • Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field
      • 1-3 years of experience in a network administration or support role
      • Strong understanding of TCP/IP, LAN/WAN, DNS, DHCP, and VPN
      • Experience with network hardware (e.g., Cisco, HP, Ubiquiti)
      • Familiarity with Windows Server and/or Linux environments
      • Strong analytical and communication skills
      • Ability to work independently and prioritize tasks effectively

    • Preferable Qualifications and Skills

      • Bachelor's degree (if not already required)
      • Certifications: CompTIA Network+ or CCNA
      • Additional certifications: CCNP, Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator, or similar
      • Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
      • Knowledge of virtualization (VMware, Hyper-V)
      • Familiarity with network monitoring tools (e.g., SolarWinds, Wireshark)
      • Scripting knowledge (PowerShell, Bash)
      • Exposure to ITIL, Agile, or project management practices

  2. Systems Engineer

    • Reference Number: SE7M5
    • Salary: $100,000 - $130,000 AUD (source)
    • Years of experience: MINIMUM 3 years

    • Brief Description

      A systems engineer is a professional who applies engineering principles to develop and maintain efficient systems.

      They play a crucial role in various industries by solving problems in systems.

      Unlike other engineers who solve issues in independent parts, a systems engineer takes a holistic approach by studying the interconnections of systems.

      When a systems engineer develops a system, they ensure it provides a positive user experience while considering the company objectives and operations.

    • Responsibilities of a Systems Engineer at CORPORATION

      1. System Design and Architecture
        • Analyze user and business requirements to design scalable systems
        • Develop system architecture and specifications for hardware and software
        • Create system models, diagrams, and documentation

      2. System Integration
        • Integrate hardware, software, and network components into a cohesive system
        • Ensure compatibility across platforms and technologies
        • Coordinate with vendors and developers to support integration tasks

      3. Installation, Configuration, and Deployment
        • Install and configure operating systems, servers, and system applications
        • Deploy solutions in line with project requirements and timelines
        • Ensure systems are set up according to organizational standards

      4. Monitoring and Maintenance
        • Monitor system performance and availability
        • Perform regular maintenance and updates to ensure reliability
        • Tune systems to enhance performance and reduce downtime

      5. Troubleshooting and Issue Resolution
        • Diagnose system-related issues and identify root causes
        • Implement fixes and recommend permanent solutions
        • Support users and technical staff in resolving escalated issues

      6. Security and Compliance
        • Implement security measures like firewalls, access controls, and encryption
        • Ensure systems comply with internal policies and external regulations (e.g., ISO, NIST)
        • Regularly patch vulnerabilities and perform risk assessments

      7. Documentation and Reporting
        • Document configurations, procedures, and system architectures
        • Maintain records of system updates, incidents, and changes
        • Prepare reports for audits, reviews, and internal tracking

      8. Automation and Optimization
        • Use scripting tools (e.g., PowerShell, Python) to automate system tasks
        • Improve existing processes to boost system efficiency and reliability

      9. Project Participation and Collaboration
        • Collaborate with developers, IT teams, and stakeholders on projects
        • Contribute to the planning and implementation of infrastructure upgrades
        • Support Agile or DevOps methodologies as applicable

      10. Continuous Improvement and Learning
        • Stay current with emerging technologies and best practices
        • Evaluate new tools and platforms for potential use
        • Participate in training, certifications, and professional development

    • Key Systems Engineer Skills and Requirements

      • Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking: Developing innovative solutions to complex challenges by analyzing systems holistically.

      • Effective Communication: Clearly conveying technical information to diverse stakeholders, facilitating collaboration and ensuring project alignment.

      • Time Management and Prioritization: Balancing multiple projects and deadlines efficiently to maintain productivity.

      • Adaptability and Flexibility: Adjusting to evolving technologies and project requirements in a dynamic environment.

      • Systems Architecture Design and Modeling: Creating and analyzing system structures to ensure they meet functional and performance requirements.

      • Programming and Scripting Languages: Proficiency in languages such as Python, Java, or C++ for system development and automation.

      • Network Configuration and Security: Understanding network protocols and implementing security measures to protect system integrity.

      • Cloud Infrastructure and Services: Experience with cloud platforms and services to design scalable and reliable systems.

      • Automation and Orchestration Tools: Utilizing tools to automate system processes and improve efficiency.

      • Continuous Learning: Staying updated with technological advancements to incorporate new solutions and maintain system relevance.
