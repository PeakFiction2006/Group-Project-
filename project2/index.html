Our System

🌐 Digital Protections

Hackers most often exploit a security breach in your systems to get an opening into your most sensitive of data. To prevent this we first start out with a digital sweep of your current systems to analyse any potential or current breaches. Then it is our porogative to patch them up and inform our clients. Moving on we require all devices to have installed the same digital protections on all devices with access to company data. Without giving an exhaustive list we provide: firewall, anti-virus, phishing protection, encryption, etc... No device with access to company data should be allowed into company servers, and that is hard coded into permissions of access for the company's server. If the company is assailed by hackers often, we provide monthly scans of all devices and servers issued with a complete report. Furthermore, our staff will attempt penetration tests to improve security systems in a timely fashion every month.

💻 Device Protections

What is the point in a strong digital protection, when your employees have the passwords to their devices written on a post it note? We offer to our serious clients multifactor authentication services. Wherein each employees devices are only accessible by them and and higher-up management. Ideally we further get rid off passwords to instead involve bio encryption to access the device itself. Furthermore, to prevent espionage theft through internal staff, we also provide heirarchical encryption. We provide everyone with a security clearance ID. Depending on how high you are in the company the more security clearances you have. And to get access to certain shared file servers on their device it requires them to input their ID, if they have the clearance access is provided.

👤 People Protections

It is often the case that despite heavy online and device securities applied leaks still occur. This is due to human error on the side of the company's employees. Giving away info that may seem innocuous but gives away enough for coorparate spies to infiltrate the company and obtain what data they so please. For instance if a person on the phone asks about applying to the firm over a phone call and asks "are there any good places for coffee?", well that gives away info of where the employees usually take their breaks and if their security card is on their person, there are devices small enough to fit onto their hand to copy said data on the card to make a copy and access the company devices. This form of data breach is highly studied in a form of hacking called "Social Engineering". At Victoria Digital Security we train employees against these forms of social hacking, by way of seminars & live demonstrations at the most basic. If the company feels the training is yet sufficient we can offer unannounced data breach tests and submit an extensive report on areas of improvement. If the company structure requires the cycling of old to new workers, we offer monthly training services to ensure the most complete data security from your workers.
