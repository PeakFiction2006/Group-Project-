About Us

We are company based from Melbourne Australia in the state of Victoria. The company was founded in the year 2000 by Ceo, Cfo & Coo respectively, Lachlan Philips, Matthew Yeo & Marcus Au. We specialise in organising entertainment services for formal or informal events. Whether it be standup-comedy, live acrobatics, in person theatre, etc... we can find the right people for the job. Here you can learn more about our mission and values, and check out the links to meet our team members.

Entertainment Services Provided


Standup Comedy

Standup comedy is the s a performance directed to a live audience, where the performer stands on a stage and delivers humorous and satirical monologues. We usually contract to local comedians in your area, however also provide the option of contacting a reknowned comedian of your choosing provided the customer can cover his or her expenses, otherwise it is a flat fee of $30 an hour.


Musical Performances(singers, Mcees, Rappers,etc...)

Musical entertainers we contract can range in genre from rap, pop, rock or jazz. There are two kinds of performers, cover acts or original composers. So If you have a specific request of songs to be played then it is suggested to inquire for a cover act. The fee for both is $100 an hour.


Stage Plays

A theatre troupe of trained actors will perform musicals or plays of your choice. However, be advised that you will need to inform them a month beforehand so they can prepare the act and whatever props and setpieces are required. An extra fee will be incurred to offset the cost of whatever props and costumes were needed to be prepared beforehand for the performance. Costs of the fee subject to change according to the complexity of the design. As of current, their service fee is upfront $2000.


Live Acrobatics

Pole platforms, tightrope and any other surfaces will be set up on the stage. Then professional seasoned acrobats will perform their 2 hour routine. For this they will require an upfront fee of $2000.

In Person Cinema

The company will bring over a cinema quality projector. Then installs a a cinema sized fabric screen at the customers direction. The desired film to be screened must be informed to the company beforehand. We will acquire the rights to distribute the film for your event. We also offer reguler 4k, VR or 3D versions of the film. We charge a flat fee of $1000 for everything involved.
